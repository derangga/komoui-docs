Welcome to the documentation for **KomoUI** (Kotlin Modern UI), a **Jetpack Compose** and **Kotlin Multiplatform** UI library that brings a collection of reusable, highly customizable, and accessible components to your apps.

KomoUI is a **100% open-source**, MIT-licensed project. It is **independent and not affiliated** with [shadcn/ui](https://ui.shadcn.com/) — it is a port inspired by shadcn/ui's clean, modern aesthetic, adapted for Jetpack Compose and Kotlin Multiplatform. We sincerely thank the shadcn/ui authors for open-sourcing the original components, which made this port possible. KomoUI is designed to be easily integrated into your projects by simply adding it to your Gradle dependencies, giving you seamless access to all the components.
